WristMarks - Privacy Policy

Effective: 28 June 2026

WristMarks is a personal watch‑collection tracker. Your collection stays on your device. We have no servers that store your data, no user accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and we never sell or share your information.

What you create stays yours

Watch details, photos, wear logs, notes, ratings, and service records are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync (off by default), this data syncs through your own Apple iCloud account so it appears on your other devices - the developer has no access to it. Apple's handling of iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Location (optional)

If you allow it, WristMarks uses your approximate location solely to show local weather that helps you pick a watch. Your coordinates are sent to Open‑Meteo (weather) and OpenStreetMap / Nominatim (to name your city). These requests aren't stored by the developer or tied to your identity. You can deny or disable location at any time; the app works without it.

Notifications

WristMarks schedules local notifications on your device only (daily wear reminder, service reminders). Nothing is sent to a server.

Photos

Photos you add are chosen through Apple's system photo picker and stored within the app's data (and your iCloud if sync is on). They are not uploaded anywhere else.

No third‑party tracking

WristMarks contains no advertising or analytics SDKs and collects no data for tracking across apps or websites.

Data retention & deletion

Your data remains until you delete individual items, use Settings → Delete All Data, or delete the app. Turning off iCloud Sync stops syncing.

Children

WristMarks is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

Third‑party services

Apple iCloud, Open‑Meteo, and OpenStreetMap (used as described above).

Changes

We'll update this policy as needed and revise the date above. A future version may add an optional AI photo‑recognition feature; this policy will be updated to describe it before that ships.
