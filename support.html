Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?

Everything lives on your device. There are no accounts and no servers that hold your collection. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Can I back up or move my collection?

Settings → Data → Export & Import exports your full collection (watches, wear logs, service records, notes, scores) as a CSV you can save or import on another device.

How do I lock the app with Face ID / Touch ID?

Settings → Security. If biometrics fail, your device passcode works as a fallback.

I'm not getting the daily wear reminder.

Enable it in Settings → Notifications, and make sure notifications are allowed for WristMarks in the iOS Settings app. Also check that a Focus or Scheduled Summary isn't holding it back.

I sold/gifted a watch - how do I remove it without losing its history?

Open the watch → ⋯ → Archive (add a reason). Archived watches leave your active collection but keep their history. Restore or permanently delete them from Settings → Data → Archived Watches.

Does WristMarks track me or show ads?

No. No ads, no analytics, no tracking. Location is used only to show local weather and is never stored by the developer.
